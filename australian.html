Australian Shepherd

Appearance

Height: Males 20-23 in (51-58 cm), Females 18-21 in (46-53 cm) Weight: 35-70 lb (16-32 kg) Coat: Medium-length double coat, weather-resistant, can be straight or wavy with a dense undercoat Colors: Blue merle, red merle, black, red, often with white and tan markings Eyes: Blue, brown, amber, or heterochromatic Tail: Naturally bobbed or docked in some working lines Ears: Set high, triangular, and tipped forward when alert

Temperament and Personality

Australian Shepherds are versatile, loyal, and highly intelligent Canine Bible +1 : Energetic, playful, and adaptive Strong herding instinct may lead them to herd children or other pets Loyal and protective of family, often forming strong bonds Can be wary of strangers but generally friendly with socialization Require mental stimulation to prevent boredom and destructive behavior Highly trainable with positive reinforcement
